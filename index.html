What PaddyLink Is

PaddyLink is a listing and discovery service operated by Kalbantt Tech OPC Pvt Ltd. It shows your paddy to more than one verified buyer. It does not buy, sell, store, or take ownership of any paddy. The sale happens directly between farmer and buyer.
